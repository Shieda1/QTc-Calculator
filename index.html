What is the QTc calculator?
The QTc calculator is a tool that performs a correction of QT interval for a patients heart rate.
How to calculate QTc?
To calculate QTc you should use the following formula:
QTc = QT + 0.154 * (1 - RR)
Why use QTc calculator?
The QTc calculator is used in order to perform a correction of QT interval for a patients heart rate.
